The greatest gift that Jehovah gave us is being able to worship and believe in him

Posted by Cristina Villanueva on November 2016

Our faith does not requires to be strong in order to become a true servant of God. We learn, we adapt, and we use our thinking ability to build up trust, faith and loyalty to the one who made us, Jehovah. In order to raise our faith, we must learn how to voice out our questions and never let our doubts be kept in our mind.

Introvert persons are persons to be beware of. They kept things for themselves to handle and many ends up being depressed, down, and even sometimes, they commit suicide. This is due to the lack of faith and the product of being alone.

However, Jehovah's Witnesses provide guidance in these particular aspects. A sign of being a servant of Jehovah, is applying all that is learned throughout the congretation. Showing that you are a servant of Jehovah is not just a coat to be removed after nobody else is present.

We all have weaknesses. Especially when it comes to the word of God. In our age today, we only care about being successful. Not spiritually, but financially. We want to have better lifestyles. Wherein, that is not a true success. Success is the satisfaction we get from helping others. Not just helping them in hard times, but guiding them through the right passage to Jehovah. That is true love. That is happiness.
